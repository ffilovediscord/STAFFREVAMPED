FAQs

Q: What if I’m unsure how to handle something?
A: Always ask a senior moderator or admin. It’s better to pause than to take action you regret later.

Q: How do I report a staff member breaking the rules?
A: Provide evidence and privately contact an Admin or the Owner.

Q: Can I take breaks from staffing?
A: Yes. Please notify the team in staff channels if you’ll be inactive so others can cover.

Q: What if I accidentally break a rule?
A: Own it and report the incident. Honesty helps maintain trust in the team.
