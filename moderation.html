Moderation Tips

  • 📌 Stay calm and neutral when resolving conflicts. Emotion-driven responses often escalate issues.
  • 🧾 Log every punishment action in the appropriate staff channel, even if it’s minor. Transparency is key.
  • 📷 Collect evidence whenever possible before acting. Screenshots or logs help justify decisions later.
  • 🛠️ Use available moderation tools like slowmode, mute timers, or auto-responses to ease your workload.
